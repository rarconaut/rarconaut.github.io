All content, all the time.

BOTTLE & PAPER BANKS

There are Bottle and Paper Banks at the Village Hall at Tixall, and by the Memorial Playing Fields at Great Haywood.

POLICE:

Ingestre and Tixall fall within the Stafford Area for policing, based at Eastgate Street, Stafford. This covers a large area.  We have one dedicated Police officer, PC Mark Robinson and two Police Community support Officers: PCSO Neil Williams and PCSO Richard Owen. They can be contacted;-

  1. By ringing 101
  2. Via Eastgate Street Police Station
  3. by email:  neil.williams@staffordshire.pnn.police.uk
  4. In an emergency ring 999 as usual

POSTAL COLLECTIONS:

The box at Ingestre Village is emptied at 8am and 4.15pm on weekdays, at 8am on Saturdays.The nearest Sunday & Bank Holiday Collections are at midday from Gt Haywood or Baswich Post Offices. The box at Tixall is emptied at 4.15pm on weekdays, and at 8.15am on Saturdays.
